FALL IN LOVE WITH NATURE

Nature is all around us both underwater and on land from the largest whale shark to the smallest coral polyp. You are going to see them all. If you love adventure than you are going to love our trips.

Explore the world

We have traveled near and far. Want to do a muck dive in the Philippines, or a drift dive in Cozumel, or a wall dive in Grand Cayman. Then come along with us.

Meet nature

Ever seen a spotted eagle ray or numerous sea turtles on a single dive? We have so come meet these beautiful creatures on one of our exotic trips.

Find your way

Want to be a better diver then get in the water and dive. Expert instruction offered on all trips from either the dive center we dive with or a club instructor.

PHOTOGRAPHY OF OUR ADVENTURES

photos by Steve

Underwater photography has been a passion of mine since 1980 when I snapped my first underwater photo using a Nikonos film camera and no strobe. Technology has changed a whole lot in the past 40 years. Instant results with digital along with much better cameras and strobes has improved my photographs immensely. I hope you enjoy the photos.

Patti's Photography

I have always loved searching for the small stuff on the reef and with that passion came my love of macro-photography. I started underwater photography about 3 years ago on a trip to Roatan. From that very first shot I was hooked and cannot get enough photographing the small stuff from muck diving in the Philippines to catching a blenny on a drift dive in Cozumel. Enjoy my shots.

Instructor Corner

Scuba diving is an activity where the margin of error can be very small. To help reduce risk divers need to keep up on skills and evaluate their physical conditioning before they hit the water. I intend to use this space to post articles that I think will enhance your joy of diving and instill confidence in your abilities. Remember if you feel your skills are rusty see a dive professional to refresh your abilities.
